Dr. Fabian M. Dayrit

Professor Emeritus of Ateneo de Manila University

Who is Dr. Fabian Dayrit?

  • 1975 - Graduated cum laude from the Ateneo de Manila University with a degree in BS Chemistry
  • 1978 - Received his M. A. degree in Princeton University
  • 1981 - Received his PhD degree in Princeton University
  • 1983 - started teaching at Ateneo de Manila University up to this present day
  • 1994 - established the National Chemistry Instrumentation Center (NCIC) when the first high field Fourier transform-Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectrometer and high resolution Mass Spectrometer (MS) in the country
  • 1995 - became the head of the Intergrated Chemists of the Philippines up to this present day
  • 2008 - has been a consultant on Confirmatory Drug Testing using Mass Spectrometry for the Department of Health (DOH)
  • 2009 - has been the chair for the Technical Panel for Nanotechnology of the Philippine Council for Advanced Science and Technology Research & Development of Department of Science & Technology

Dr. Dayrit is a true-blue Atenean. His great contributions to science and technology in the Philippines has earned him the title of Emeritus professor.
